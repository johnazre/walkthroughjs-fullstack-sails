WalkthroughJS

Have you ever been in front of a textbook, teacher, computer, or some combination of the three and just feel like you keep missing a step? So have I. Trying to learn Javascript without going at it at my pace was too difficult. I would always have to go back and break everything down, step by step, before I was able to understand it. I know I'm not the only one that has ever struggled like that, so I created WalkthroughJS. The goal of WalkthroughJS is to break down the concepts of Javascript into steps so you don't feel like you're constantly missing something.

This project is in its infancy, at the moment, so the content is fairly limited, however, I am looking for people who want to contribute and make this into something bigger. This is an open-source project! Help others learn to code the way you wish you could have!
